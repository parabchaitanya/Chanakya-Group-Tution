ABOUT US

CHNKYA GROUP TUITIONS was established on 20th December 1982 with a futuristic vision to provide qualitative education by applying innovative and dynamic pedagogical techniques.

Students studying with CHANKYA group of institutions are given placements in some of the leading organizations in the world. Many deserving students are absorbed as teaching faculty in CHANKYA institutes.
