general knowledge

Have you ever wondered; how people win famous reality shows like Kaun Banega Crorepati? Or how do people crack difficult entrance tests or various other competitive exams? How do people win quiz competitions? How are they so aware of their surroundings and various fields? General Knowledge is the subject that helps them in this regard. GK helps the individual to gain more and more knowledge. It helps you to remain updated and makes you so confident. It helps you to remember various events and developments. That is why we are presenting general knowledge questions for class 10. We hope that these General Knowledge questions will help in creating your base regarding various fields and will surely help you in the future. Try to solve these questions and check your general awareness. Learn the facts which you were not aware of. Keep Practicing !! Keep Learning !! General knowledge as a subject helps the students to have holistic development of their personality in terms of logical, analytical, and thinking skills. The GK subject is important for the students of class 10 as it prepares them for the future competitive exam that they might face in the future. Gk not only makes the students familiar with the knowledge about the world events, industry news, happening around us but also covers the current events that make us aware of our nearby surroundings. It provides a new view of the thought process and helps in a better perspective about things in general.
